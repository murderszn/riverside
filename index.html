Our Story

Our Family Story

Riverside Cafe began as a family dream in 2015, when we opened our doors with a simple mission: to serve the best breakfast in Lake County while creating a place where families could gather and create lasting memories. What started as a small family business has grown into a beloved community institution, but we've never lost sight of our roots.

Every morning, our family comes together to prepare fresh, made-to-order meals using the finest local ingredients. From our signature pancakes to our hearty skillets, every dish is crafted with the same love and attention to detail that we put into our own family meals. We believe that great food brings people together, and we're honored to be part of your family's traditions.

When you walk through our doors, you're not just a customer – you're part of our extended family. We look forward to welcoming you home to Riverside Cafe.
