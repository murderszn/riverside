Our Story

A warm welcome since 2015

This storied building was first designed in 2015.

Located in the heart of Gurnee, Illinois, Riverside Cafe has been serving the community with quality food and beverages for nearly a decade. We pride ourselves on using fresh, local ingredients and providing exceptional service in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Whether you're stopping by for our famous pancakes, morning coffee, enjoying lunch with friends, or simply taking a break from your busy day, Riverside Cafe is your home away from home.
